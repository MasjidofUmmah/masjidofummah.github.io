Our Story

By the grace of Allah (SWT), Masjid of Ummah was officially established on May 24th, 2024. What began as a collective vision among dedicated community members has blossomed into a permanent house of worship, learning, and gathering for Muslims in the area.

"Our mission is to cultivate a welcoming, spiritual, and educational environment where individuals and families can grow in their faith, strengthen their connection to Allah, and serve the broader community with compassion."

We believe that a masjid should be the vibrant heart of the community. Beyond offering a peaceful space for the five daily prayers and Jumu'ah, we are committed to providing robust Islamic education for our youth, regular halaqas, family nights, and outreach programs.
