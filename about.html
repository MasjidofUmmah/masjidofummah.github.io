About Masjid Ummah

Built by the Ummah, for the Ummah.

Our Story

By the grace of Allah (SWT), Masjid Ummah was officially established on September 30th, 2024. What began as a collective vision among dedicated community members has blossomed into a permanent house of worship, learning, and gathering for Muslims in the area.

"Our mission is to cultivate a welcoming, spiritual, and educational environment where individuals and families can grow in their faith, strengthen their connection to Allah, and serve the broader community with compassion."

We believe that a masjid should be the vibrant heart of the community. Beyond offering a peaceful space for the five daily prayers and Jumu'ah, we are committed to providing robust Islamic education for our youth, regular halaqas, family nights, and outreach programs. Masjid Ummah is your home away from home—a place where faith is nurtured, bonds are formed, and the prophetic tradition is practiced daily.

Our Core Programs & Services

🕌 Daily & Friday Prayers

We are open for all five daily prayers in congregation. We also hold weekly Jumu'ah services featuring inspiring and relevant khutbahs.

📖 Quran & Islamic Studies

We offer foundational Quran reading, memorization (Hifz), and Tajweed classes for youth and adults, helping our community connect with the Book of Allah.

👨‍👩‍👧‍👦 Family & Youth Nights

Engaging weekly programs designed to strengthen family bonds and provide our youth with a safe, Islamic environment to learn and socialize.

🤝 Community Service

Driven by Islamic values, we organize food drives, charity collections, and outreach initiatives to support our neighbors and those in need.

💍 Nikah Services

We offer Islamic marriage (Nikah) officiation to help couples begin their lives together in accordance with the Sunnah. Please contact us for scheduling and requirements.
